"Tom Waits is a polarizing artist. To some people, he’s one of the greatest songwriters of the last half century. A true poet and storyteller of the rock and roll era. An ageless artist who has endured without diminishing for four decades. One of modern music’s most unique, most thunderous characters. Or he’s a pretentious, overrated phony with a fedora and soul patch who imitates bad jazz music for white people who don’t actually like jazz music. Although I know a lot of fellow Tom Waits fans, I know even more who “want” to like Tom Waits. He’s not a guy with traditional hits, so knowing where to dive in can be a challenge. Hopefully this will give you enough of a framework to get started."
