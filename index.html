Who is Tom Waits?

For close to 50 years, Tom Waits has brought his own unique set of musical and acting skills to the world. From the world of 'down and out' bars, to the 'clang and clunk' of junkyard instrumentalisation, each fan of Tom has their own favourite period.

Whether it's your first taste of Tom, or you are a seasoned fan, you can get 'all things Tom' here.
