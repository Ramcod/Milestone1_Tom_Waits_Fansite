Who Is Tom Waits?

For close to 50 years, Tom Waits has brought his own unique set of musical and acting skills to the world. From the world of 'down and out' bars, to the 'clang and clunk' of junkyard instrumentalisation, each fan of Tom has their own favourite period. Whether it's your first taste of Tom, or you are a seasoned fan, you can get 'all things Tom' on the following pages. If you subscribe you'll also receive all of the latest news and can vote for your favourite Tom tune!

Rumours of a new album 22.10.2021

Hold on to your hats, there are rumours of a new album for 2022. It's not clear if this is new material, cover versions, or re-issues, but at this stage we will take what we can get! A source close to Tom suspects that it will indeed be some covers from Tom's inspirations including the folk of Woody Guthrie and the blues of Howlin' Wolf
